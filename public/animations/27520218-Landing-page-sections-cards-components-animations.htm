    CPI control, audience cards, and stats for a research panel

    A set of landing page sections for a market research and survey panel platform.

    The blocks cover the core story a researcher needs before signing up: pricing and CPI control with a live offerwall position preview, audience cards tailored to research agencies, universities, and marketing teams, and a stats row showing reach — 16 million registered respondents, 82 countries, 32 languages.

    Kept the data visual and clear so each benefit reads in a glance. Purple and orange run through the whole set to tie the sections together.

    Built in Figma. Open to feedback, and available for new projects.
